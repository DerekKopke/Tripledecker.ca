Getting to know you

10/18/2016

 
Business around the globe:

I’ve had many interesting stories regarding business travel, some more harrowing than others. Having visited more than 65 countries globally, I can tell you that people around the world share many common traits among their differences. It is understanding the customs and formalities of cultures globally that often dictate whether or not you will find success in that particular region.

At Triple Decker Enterprises, we focus on understanding your business and finding the best way to implement your technology and/or product in multiple markets globally. It is this very understanding of the global market which has helped us make an impact from the smallest start-up to multi-national publicly traded companies.

Let us be a part of your future success.

Derek Kopke - Founder - Triple Decker Enterprises
